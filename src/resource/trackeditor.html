Modesto- The Track Editor

Trk The trackname
Vol Volume of this track.
Pan Pan to left or right.
in A MIDI input port from where to record.
out A MIDI output port where to play.
Chn A MIDI Output Channel from 1 to 16.
Bnk Bank Select
Ie. internal sounds vs. cartridge.
Prg Program
Ie. which sound to use on this channel.
Map An alternative mapping of the keys.
Ie. in case you need another layout for a drumset to make playing more fun or to reach sounds you could not play otherwise because your keyboard is too small.
